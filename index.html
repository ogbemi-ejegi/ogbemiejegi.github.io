Startup Valuation Prediction

I analyzed the dataset of unicorn companies and startup across the globe and built a machine learning model to predict whether a company will reach a valuation of over $5 billion. I prepared the data for modeling by exploring and cleaning the data which included columns such as country of origin, sector, investors and valuation. I then performed exploratory data analysis to understand the data better before going on to training the data. Finally, I predicted whether a unicorn company will reach a valuation of over $5 billion and assessed the confidence in the results. The model was able to predict with an accuracy of 85%. This information will be valuable to investment teams, as it will help them to make more informed decisions about which companies to invest in.
